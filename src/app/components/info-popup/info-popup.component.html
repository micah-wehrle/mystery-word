How Wordle Works:

Your goal is to guess the 5-letter word using the letters in the provided ransom note. Be warned! You can only use each letter cutout once. If the ransom note contains the letter 'e' 4 times, and you guess 'steam', then you can only use 'e' 3 more times.

Guess Colors:

When you make a guess, the boxes behind the letters will turn one of three colors:

  • Black: The letter you guessed is not in the word.
  • Yellow: The letter you guessed is in the word, but not in the right place.
  • Green: The letter you guessed is both in the word, and in the right place!
